# Supply Chain Audit: Profit Recovery & Logistics Optimization 📈

## 📌 Project Overview
An executive-level data audit of over **180,000 supply chain transactions**. This project diagnoses the root causes of margin erosion and a reported **54.8% delay rate**, providing data-driven solutions to stabilize profitability and operational efficiency.

## 📊 Key Insights & Results
* **The Discount Trap:** Identified a strong **-0.82 negative correlation** between discounts and profit. High discounts (>30%) were the primary driver of net losses.
* **The Scheduling Fallacy:** Discovered that the high delay rate was an "artificial failure." The actual shipping gap was only **0.51 days**, indicating unrealistic system scheduling rather than operational inefficiency.
* **Profit Engines:** Classified **Fishing** and **Camping & Hiking** as high-margin resilient categories for strategic investment.

## 🚀 Strategic Recommendations
* **Margin Protection:** Implement a strict **20% discount cap** to recover lost net margins.
* **Logistics Re-calibration:** Add a **24-hour buffer** to delivery estimates to improve On-Time Delivery KPIs by **~40%**.
* **Budget Reallocation:** Shift marketing spend toward identified "Profit Engine" categories to maximize ROI.
